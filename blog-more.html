The Sea is Calling

Sitting serenely on the turquoise coasts of the Caribbean Sea lie two properties that embody paradise in quiet luxury. Park Hyatt St. Kitts and Andaz Mayakoba are the answers to your desires for a sun-filled ocean getaway. Whether vacationing with the whole family, or lounging your time away with a loved one, both properties capture the essence of resort living on beautiful grounds with unforgettable itineraries.

Park Hyatt ST. KIitts

Amidst sugar mill ruins and grassy sloping peaks, Park Hyatt St. Kitts brings a sense of rich historic charm to sandy beaches and coastal living that one rarely finds elsewhere. Thin palms careen and crane throughout the shoreline as thick, blooming vegetation continues to the summit of each mountain. Park Hyatt St. Kitts is strategically placed to not only capture the essence of the island, but to allow guests to discover more through aquatic adventures, thoughtful dining experiences, and more.
